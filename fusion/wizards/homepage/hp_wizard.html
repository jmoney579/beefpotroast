Touts and Broadcasts

Touts or VDSS News articles are now located at the top of the homepage and broadcasts beneath. All users now have the ability to submit touts to Public Affairs for review and posting. You can do so by hovering over the touts links in the main navigation and then selecting Tout Requests.

Navigation

Navigation throughout Fusion utilizes a meganav style presentation. This style of navigation is horizontal in nature and allows for the display of links to all pages when visiting a division's section of the site. If you click on one of the links to a division under the division's dropdown, you will be presented with that division's section which will display their the pages grouped by category. To return to the full list of divisions, click the back button.

Search

The search features in Fusion have been dramatically improved. Searching now occurs on the fly, as you type, and includes not only the content within a page, but also files and the content within them. The display has been streamlined and categorized to allow for easier access to the requested content.
